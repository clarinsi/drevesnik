## About the tool

Drevesnik is an online application for querying Slovenian dependency-parsed corpora. Its primary goal is to support researchers in exploring a wide range of grammatical phenomena in Slovenian. Users begin by entering a [search query](https://orodja.cjvt.si/drevesnik/help/en/) and selecting the desired corpora. The results are displayed as dependency trees (graphs) and can also be downloaded for more detailed analysis.

Drevesnik supports querying several manually and automatically annotated corpora, with additional corpora easily added. Most corpora follow the cross-linguistically standardized <a href="https://universaldependencies.org/">Universal Dependencies</a> annotation scheme, which includes universal part-of-speech tags, morphosyntactic features, and dependency relations. The corpora also features lemmas and morphosyntactic tags based on the <a href="https://wiki.cjvt.si/books/04-oblikoskladnja-multext-east">MULTEXT-East</a> annotation scheme.

The interface is based on the open-source <a href="https://github.com/TurkuNLP/dep_search">dep_search</a> tool, developed by the University of Turku, which was localized and further developed with support from the CLARIN.SI research infrastructure (2019 CLARIN.SI Projects) and the Slovenian Research and Innovation Agency (<a href="https://spot.ff.uni-lj.si">SPOT</a> Z6-4617, <a href="https://www.ff.uni-lj.si/mapcase">MAPCASE</a> J6-70213, PJVT P6-0411).

The code is freely available in the <a href="https://github.com/clarinsi/drevesnik">CLARIN.SI GitHub repository</a>.
